While the following represent some of the core technologies and ecosystems we frequently deploy, our expertise is not limited to these alone. At SWTech, we have the capability to meet you where your technology stack is currently and help you transition to where you want it to go. 

Our vast experience across diverse technical environments allows us to get up to speed rapidly on your existing infrastructure, providing immediate value whether we are troubleshooting an old system or planning a complete modernization.

---

### ![UniFi Logo](/assets/images/logos/unifi.png) Ubiquiti / UniFi
**Enterprise-Grade Networking for Everyone**

Ubiquiti’s UniFi ecosystem is our preferred choice for robust networking. It allows us to deploy high-capacity Wi-Fi, secure firewalls, and managed switches that can be monitored remotely. Whether it's a small home or a multi-acre farm, UniFi provides the scalability and reliability required for modern connectivity.

### ![Home Assistant Logo](/assets/images/logos/home-assistant.png) Home Assistant
**Privacy-First Home Automation**

Home Assistant is the world’s most powerful open-source home automation platform. We use it to integrate thousands of different devices—from lighting and climate control to solar monitoring—into a single, unified interface. Unlike many "cloud-only" solutions, Home Assistant runs locally, ensuring your data stays private and your home stays smart even if the internet goes down.

### ![Starlink Logo](/assets/images/logos/starlink.png) Starlink
**Revolutionary Satellite Internet**

For our rural and remote clients, Starlink has been a game-changer. We specialize in the professional installation of Starlink hardware, including custom mounting solutions and integration with high-performance networking gear to ensure consistent, high-speed internet across your entire property.

### ![NBN Logo](/assets/images/logos/nbn.png) Australian NBN
**Connecting Australian Homes and Businesses**

We work with all types of NBN connections (FTTP, FTTN, Fixed Wireless) to optimize your internal network. From troubleshooting speed issues to installing structured cabling that ensures your NBN connection reaches every corner of your building, we make sure your "last mile" is as fast as possible.

### ![Reolink Logo](/assets/images/logos/reolink.png) Reolink
**Smart Security & Surveillance**

Reolink provides a versatile and cost-effective range of security solutions. From 4K PoE (Power over Ethernet) cameras to solar-powered 4G units for remote farm gates, Reolink systems offer excellent image quality and smart person/vehicle detection without the burden of monthly subscription fees.
